Frequently Asked Questions

How long do the games take?

Most games take between 5-15 minutes to complete, with some longer story-based games taking up to 30 minutes.

Are my results private?

Yes, all your results are private by default. You can choose to share them if you want to show off your personality insights.

Can I retake the assessments?

Absolutely! You can retake any assessment to see how your results change over time or to explore different response patterns.
