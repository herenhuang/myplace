# Quiz Type Selector - Which Should I Use?

Quick guide to help you choose between **Archetype**, **Story-Matrix**, and **Narrative** quizzes.

---

## üéØ Archetype Quiz (Fixed Personalities)

### Use When:
- ‚úÖ Matching to **existing characters** (e.g., "Which Naruto character are you?")
- ‚úÖ Matching to **established types** (e.g., "Which Hogwarts house?")
- ‚úÖ You have **4-8 distinct, pre-defined personas**
- ‚úÖ Results should be **instantly recognizable** (e.g., Myers-Briggs types)

### Characteristics:
- **Outcomes**: 4-8 fixed results
- **Questions**: 6-10, can be preferences or scenarios
- **Story Arc**: None - questions are independent
- **Scoring**: Manual point assignment per answer
- **AI Role**: Optional explanation only
- **Setup Time**: ‚≠ê‚≠ê 30-60 minutes

### Examples:
- "Which AI Model Are You?" ‚Üí Claude, GPT-4, Gemini
- "Which Disney Princess?" ‚Üí Ariel, Belle, Mulan
- "Which Hogwarts House?" ‚Üí Gryffindor, Slytherin, etc.

---

## üåü Story-Matrix Quiz (Disconnected Scenarios)

### Use When:
- ‚úÖ Exploring **open-ended traits** (e.g., "What's your travel style?")
- ‚úÖ You want **100+ unique outcomes** (10√ó10 matrix)
- ‚úÖ Results should be **personalized combinations** (e.g., "Bold Foodie")
- ‚úÖ Users should feel **uniquely seen** (not bucketed)

### Characteristics:
- **Outcomes**: 100 combinations (10 first √ó 10 second words)
- **Questions**: 8+, each is independent scenario
- **Story Arc**: None - each question standalone
- **Scoring**: AI analyzes all answers
- **AI Role**: Critical - selects result AND writes explanation
- **Setup Time**: ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê 1-2 hours

### Examples:
- "What's Your Vacation Style?" ‚Üí Spontaneous Foodie, Structured Planner
- "What's Your Learning Style?" ‚Üí Visual Practitioner, Auditory Theorist

---

## üé¨ Narrative Quiz (Continuous Story)

### Use When:
- ‚úÖ You want users to **LIVE THROUGH a story**
- ‚úÖ Natural **timeline exists** (days, weeks, phases)
- ‚úÖ **Recurring characters** add depth
- ‚úÖ Context builds over time (management, relationships, crises)
- ‚úÖ Choices should feel **consequential** within a journey

### Characteristics:
- **Outcomes**: 100 combinations (same as story-matrix)
- **Questions**: 10 sequential scenes in ONE story
- **Story Arc**: Full narrative arc with beginning/middle/end
- **Scoring**: AI analyzes journey through specific story
- **AI Role**: Adapts each scene based on previous choices
- **Setup Time**: ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê 2-3 hours

### Examples:
- "Your First 2 Weeks as Manager" ‚Üí Story adapts to your choices
- "7 Days Solo in Tokyo" ‚Üí Trip unfolds based on decisions
- "First Month Dating" ‚Üí Relationship evolves with your actions

## üí° Real-World Scenarios

### Scenario 1: "Which Pok√©mon type are you?"
**Choose**: Archetype
**Why**: 18 pre-defined types. Users expect "Fire" not "Energetic Fire Lover"

**Choose**: Story-Matrix
**Why**: Travel is nuanced. "Spontaneous Foodie" vs "Structured Nature Lover" - but no natural single narrative

**Choose**: Narrative
**Why**: Perfect timeline (7 days), recurring team members, decisions build on each other

**Choose**: Archetype

**Choose**: Story-Matrix
**Why**: Multi-dimensional but no single story - "Direct Truth Seeker" from various scenarios

### Scenario 6: "48 hours handling a crisis"
**Choose**: Narrative
**Why**: Clear timeline, building stakes, your choices affect next moments

### Scenario 7: "What's your creative process?"
**Choose**: Story-Matrix
**Why**: Creative process is situational, doesn't need one continuous story

### Scenario 8: "Your first month dating someone new"
**Choose**: Narrative
**Why**: Clear progression (first date ‚Üí texting ‚Üí meeting friends), recurring person

## üé® Quick Start Guide by Type

### Archetype Quiz:
1. List 4-8 personalities
2. Create/find images
3. Write 6-10 questions (4 options each)
4. Design scoring rules
**Time**: 30-60 minutes

### Story-Matrix Quiz:
1. Identify core dimensions
2. Create 10 first words (approaches)
3. Create 10 second words (priorities)  
4. Write 8 disconnected scenarios
5. Craft AI prompts
**Time**: 1-2 hours

### Narrative Quiz:
1. **READ NARRATIVE_QUIZ_CREATION.md**
2. Design your story arc
3. Create 3-5 recurring characters
4. Write story setup (premise, stakes)
5. Write 10 base scenarios (chronological)
6. Create word matrix
7. Craft AI prompts for adaptation + results
**Time**: 2-3 hours

## üéØ When to Choose Narrative Over Story-Matrix

Both give 100 combinations, so how to choose?

### Choose Story-Matrix If:
- ‚ùå No natural timeline (e.g., "creative style" has no chronology)
- ‚ùå Questions are naturally independent
- ‚ùå No recurring characters make sense
- ‚ùå You want simpler implementation
- ‚ùå Faster quiz (no AI calls during)

### Choose Narrative If:
- ‚úÖ Clear timeline (days, weeks, phases)
- ‚úÖ Recurring characters add depth
- ‚úÖ Context builds meaningfully
- ‚úÖ Decisions should feel connected
- ‚úÖ You want maximum immersion
- ‚úÖ Story enriches the insights

**Example**: 
- "What's your work style?" ‚Üí **Story-Matrix** (no single story needed)
- "Your first 90 days at a new job" ‚Üí **Narrative** (perfect timeline)

## üö® Common Mistakes by Type

### Archetype Mistakes:
- ‚ùå Too many personalities (10+)
- ‚ùå Biased scoring (everyone gets same result)
- ‚ùå No variety in results

### Story-Matrix Mistakes:
- ‚ùå Words too similar ("Explorer" and "Adventurer")
- ‚ùå Questions don't map to dimensions
- ‚ùå Too few questions for 100 combinations

### Narrative Mistakes:
- ‚ùå Vague scenes without immersion
- ‚ùå Timeline doesn't make sense
- ‚ùå Characters inconsistent or forgettable
- ‚ùå No story arc (just 10 random scenes)
- ‚ùå Base scenarios not adaptable

---

## üéØ Final Recommendation

**Start with Archetype** if:
- First quiz ever
- Clear pre-defined results
- Want something simple

**Use Story-Matrix** when:
- Exploring personality/behavior
- Want 100+ outcomes
- Scenarios are naturally independent

**Use Narrative** when:
- Natural timeline exists
- Story enhances the experience
- Ready for advanced immersion
- Worth the extra effort

All three are powerful! Choose based on your topic and how much time you have. üöÄ
